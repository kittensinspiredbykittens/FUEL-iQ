Our principles

What we believe about youth nutrition

These aren't just features — they're commitments baked into every part of FuelIQ.

Never ask for weight

Guidance is based on age, sport, and training load only. Body weight has no place in youth athlete nutrition tracking.

Fuel, not restriction

All AI output frames nutrition around energy, performance, and recovery — never calories to cut or foods to avoid.

Parents as partners

Youth athletes need the adults in their lives on their side. FuelIQ puts parents in the loop with language they can use.

Sport-specific by default

A swimmer and a soccer player have different needs. FuelIQ knows the difference and adjusts accordingly.
